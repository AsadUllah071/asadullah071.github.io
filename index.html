Lets Begin with my Little Journey

My educational journey began with a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Sciences from Bahria University Islamabad, where I completed my thesis study on "Drinking Water Quality Assessment in Selected Areas of Rawalpindi". During my undergraduate years, I delved into real-world applications through an internship project focused on 'Glacial Lake Outburst Flood (GLOF) Glacial Delineation' in the Gilgit, Hunza, and Shingo Basin. In the professional realm, I have served as an Environmental and Management Consultant at EMC, contributing to impactful projects by conducting environmental impact assessments, developing Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) and Initial Environmental Examination (IEE) reports, and ensuring compliance with environmental legislation. This website serves as a platform to share insights from my academic journey, professional experiences, and the intersection of technology and environmental science.
